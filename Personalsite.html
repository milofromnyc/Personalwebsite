Marcos A. Fernandez

My name is Marcos A. Fernandez. I was born in New York, but left the city to attend North Country School (NCS), a small boarding school in the heart of the Adirondack mountains. It was here that I found my love for the outdoors. I was able to grow up on a working farm experiencing first hand where my food comes from. This has lead me to my current pursuits of selling maple syrup and rock climbing.
